# Naming and Designing Lead Magnets

**Source:** Amy Porterfield, Alex Hormozi
**Tags:** #lead-magnets #naming #design

## Naming Your Lead Magnet

### The Title Must:
- Spark curiosity
- Explain what they'll get
- No cutesy names that don't make sense

### Three Naming Approaches

**1. The "How To" Approach (Simple and Direct)**
"How to Design Stunning Graphics"
"How to Build an Email List from Scratch"

**2. The "How I" Angle (Personal and Relatable)**
"How I Negotiated a Raise and Doubled My Income"
"How I Grew from 0 to 50,000 Subscribers"

**3. The Intriguing Question (Taps Into Pain)**
"How Secure Is Your Online Business?"
"Are You Making These 5 Email Mistakes?"

### Hormozi's Rule
The headline is 80% of your success. Test headlines, images, and sub-headlines.

### Getting Unstuck
Use ChatGPT to brainstorm 10 better titles, but make the final call yourself.

## Writing the Copy

### Be Concise and Scannable
- Use bullet points
- Subheadings
- Short paragraphs
- Keep freebies easy to digest

### Speak Their Language
Know your ideal customer like a best friend. Understand their pain points and use the exact words they use.

### The AI Rule
Use ChatGPT as a Kickstarter, but the content must come from you. Pure AI content blends in with the noise, and people will know.

## Designing the Lead Magnet

### Use Templates
Never start from scratch. Use Canva for PDFs (guides, workbooks, cheat sheets). Find a template and build your content in.

### Video Tools
Record on your iPhone, or use tools like VideoAsk.

### Imagery
- Unsplash (free high-quality stock photos)
- Social Curator (paid membership)

### Outsourcing
If you hate design, hire someone on Fiverr or 99designs to format your content into a template.

## The Tech Setup

### Landing Page
- Above the fold: opt-in form visible without scrolling
- Standalone page: remove website navigation (no other options except to sign up)
- Clear language: make the offer crystal clear

### Thank You Page
- Do NOT deliver the freebie here
- Tell them sign-up worked, express gratitude, guide them to check email
- Add teaser: "Don't skip page five, it's my favorite tip!"

### URL
Keep it simple and memorable: `yourdomain.com/leads` or `yourdomain.com/freebie-name`

## Quality Control

Before launching, have a friend go through the entire process end-to-end (click link → receive email). Ask: is anything confusing? Is anything broken?
