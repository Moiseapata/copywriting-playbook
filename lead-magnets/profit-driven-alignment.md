# Profit-Driven Lead Magnet Alignment

**Source:** Amy Porterfield (Episode 271)
**Tags:** #lead-magnets #strategy #alignment #porterfield

## The Core Mindset Shift

A lead magnet should provide incredible value **while leading your new subscriber to a buying decision sooner than later.** Most lead magnets are missing this alignment—they are not intentionally connected to what you plan to sell.

## The 3 Strategic Questions

Before creating your lead magnet, answer these:

### 1. What do you currently sell or plan to sell?
If you already sell something, this takes 10 seconds. If you don't, get clear on the direction first.

### 2. What does your ideal customer need to understand, be aware of, or believe to want your product?
This is the "magic sauce." Before someone buys, they must believe the solution is necessary and doable. Your lead magnet's job is to start that process.

### 3. What type of lead magnet could complement or align with what you sell?
Based on where you need to start the conversation (Question 2), what format will best get them there?

## The Alignment Examples

### Example 1: List Building Course
- **Sells:** List Builders Society (course on profitable email list building)
- **Needed belief:** List building is vital AND doable for me
- **Lead magnet:** "20 Smart Strategies to Rapidly Grow Your Email List" (Cheat Sheet)
- **Why it works:** Shows WHAT to do (empowering, immediate action). Course teaches HOW.

### Example 2: Weight Loss Membership
- **Sells:** Weight loss membership (not food-plan based)
- **Needed belief:** This method is NOT about food plans—it's about principles
- **Lead magnet:** "Three Things You Must Know to Lose Weight" (Mini-Course)
- **Why it works:** Teaches principles upfront. Filters for right-fit customers.

## The Conversation Continuum

Lead magnet → Weekly content (podcast, blog, video) → Paid offer

Everything should feel like one continuous conversation, not disconnected pieces.

## Key Takeaways

- You can have many valuable lead magnets, but only a few that are "profit-driven"
- It's okay to create specific lead magnets for podcast episodes that aren't designed to sell
- **Beginners:** Just get a lead magnet created and start collecting leads. Apply this intentionality as you mature.
- **Launches:** If you have a course that opens/closes on a schedule, a profit-driven lead magnet is the perfect tool to nurture leads between enrollment periods
