# Write Curiosity Subject Line Prompt

**Tags:** #prompt #email #subject-lines #curiosity #claude

## How to Use This Prompt

When you need subject lines that drive opens using curiosity gaps.

---

## The Prompt
Generate 15 curiosity-based email subject lines for [email topic/offer].

Use these formats:

Open Loop: Start a story without finishing it.

"Something strange happened when I..."
"I wasn't going to share this, but..."
Negative Curiosity: "Don't open this email"
Question Gap: A question they can't answer without opening.

"Can you eat yourself thin?"
"What do they eat in prison?"
Drama/Confession: Emotional tension.

"My confession"
"This is embarrassing"
"Bad news"
Counterintuitive: Something that shouldn't be true.

"Why introverts make the best salespeople"
Number + Curiosity: "The 3 things I never told you about [topic]"
The "Weird" Hook (Suby): "This weird [thing] we found..."
Specific Pain + Promise: Under 40 characters.

"Shy? You'll make a killing in sales"
Rules:

Keep under 9 words (mobile-friendly)
No clickbait that doesn't deliver
Use lowercase occasionally (feels more personal)
Avoid spam trigger words
For each subject line, note which psychological mechanism it uses.
