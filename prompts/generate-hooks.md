## Hook Theory Reference

- **Curiosity gap:** Humans need to close information gaps. Open one and withhold resolution.
- **Pattern interrupt:** The brain filters out the familiar. Break the pattern to get attention.
- **Identity recognition:** When someone sees themselves in the hook ("that's me"), they can't ignore it.
- **Specificity:** Specifics are believable. Vague claims are filtered as noise.
