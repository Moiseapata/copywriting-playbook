# Identity Transformation (Frank vs Matt Model)

**Source:** Andre Chaperon
**Tags:** #storytelling #identity #psychology #andre-chaperon

## Core Concept

People don't just buy products—they buy transformations in identity. The most powerful marketing lets prospects see themselves in a story and recognize "that's me" before showing them "that's who I could become."

## The Framework

### Frank = Struggling Identity (Before)
- Overwhelmed, chasing tactics
- Frustrated with lack of results
- Doubting whether success is possible for them
- "I'm a Frank"

### Matt = Successful Identity (After)
- Focused, strategic, successful
- Has clarity and confidence
- Achieved what Frank wants
- "I want to become a Matt"

## The Mechanism

1. **Tell Frank's story** so vividly that prospects think, "That's exactly me"
2. **Show Frank's transformation** into Matt
3. **Reveal the mechanism** (your product/service) that made it possible
4. **Invite them to become Matt**

## Key Insight

The naming is intentionally simple and relatable—not fancy marketing personas. Frank and Matt feel like real people, not segments.

## Example Usage

- Email sequences that follow Frank's journey
- Landing pages that speak to "Franks" directly
- Webinars structured as Frank's epiphany story
