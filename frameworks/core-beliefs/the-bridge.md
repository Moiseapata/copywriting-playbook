# The Bridge

**Source:** Sabri Suby
**Tags:** #psychology #pain #desire #core-framework

## Core Concept

Every sale is a bridge between two states:
- **Point A:** The customer's current painful situation
- **Point B:** Their desired outcome

Your product/service is the bridge. Copy's job is to make both sides of the bridge vivid and real.

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Define Point A (Pain)
- What is the explicit problem they're experiencing right now?
- What does it feel like emotionally?
- What has it cost them so far? (money, time, relationships, self-respect)
- What have they already tried that didn't work?

### Step 2: Define Point B (Desire)
- What does the ideal outcome look like?
- How will their life be different?
- What will they be able to do that they can't do now?
- Who will they become?

### Step 3: Build the Bridge (Your Solution)
- Why is your solution uniquely capable of getting them from A to B?
- What makes it different from what they've already tried?
- Why now?

## The Copy Formula

1. **Agitate the pain** (make Point A unbearable)
2. **Paint the desire** (make Point B irresistible)
3. **Position your solution** (the only bridge that works)

## Common Mistake

Starting the conversation ten steps ahead of the audience. You must meet them at their immediate pain point so they feel like you read their mind.
