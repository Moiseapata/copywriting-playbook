# Value Debt

**Source:** Alex Hormozi
**Tags:** #value-debt #offer-construction #hormozi #core-mechanism

## Core Concept

When you give away immense value for free, you put the prospect in "value debt." The psychological weight of that free gift makes them feel compelled to reciprocate—to at least reply, click, or consider your offer.

## The Mechanism

### The Principle
Give away your absolute best stuff for free. When people see the free stuff works, the perceived likelihood of the paid program working approaches 100%.

### The "Point A to Point B" Model

**Point A (Lead Magnet):** Free content that solves the first initial, specific problem.
- Example: Free guide on getting the first 1,000 followers

**Point B (Bridge to Paid):** Comprehensive free content that gives them everything to reach a significant milestone.
- Example: Full YouTube channel teaching everything to reach 50,000 followers

**Point C (Paid Offer):** Once they reach the milestone using free material, they need high-level implementation, accountability, and proximity.
- Example: Coaching to go from 50K to 500K followers

### Why This Works
- They've seen the free stuff actually works
- Trust is already built
- The next problem naturally requires paid help
- They feel indebted from all the free value

## Key Quote
"Give away a $9,500 course for free. The prospect is now in value debt. They feel compelled to at least reply to a DM."

## How to Use

1. Create free content that genuinely solves a specific problem
2. Make it better than most people's paid stuff
3. Let results speak for themselves
4. Only then present the paid offer as the natural next step
