# Give in Public, Ask in Private

**Source:** Alex Hormozi
**Tags:** #trust #content-strategy #hormozi #selling

## Core Concept

The most effective content strategy is simple: give value constantly in public, and only make asks (sell) in private channels like email and DMs. This builds massive trust while preserving the ability to monetize.

## The Principle

**Give, give, give, give, give, give until they ASK to buy.**

Your public content should be so valuable that:
- People binge it
- They implement and get results
- They naturally want more
- They reach out to YOU asking how to buy

## The Mechanism

### Public Content (The Give)
- YouTube videos, podcasts, social posts, blog articles
- Pure value, no pitch
- Positions you as the expert
- Builds trust at scale
- Creates "value debt"

### Private Channels (The Ask)
- Email sequences
- DM conversations
- Webinar registrations
- Only present offers here

## Why This Works

- Public content attracts and pre-sells
- Private asks don't dilute your public reputation
- People who join your private channels are self-selected as interested
- The ask feels earned, not spammy

## The Ratio

There is no fixed ratio. The principle is: give so much in public that when you finally ask in private, the prospect feels they owe you attention.
