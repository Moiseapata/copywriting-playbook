# Pre-Framing

**Source:** Andre Chaperon
**Tags:** #trust #belief-shifting #storytelling #andre-chaperon

## Core Concept

Do not send cold traffic directly to sales pages. First send them to belief-shifting content that reshapes how they think about the problem. By the time they reach the offer, they're already aligned and far more likely to convert.

## The Problem with Direct Selling

Cold traffic arrives with:
- Skepticism
- Past failures
- Competing beliefs
- No trust in you

A sales page alone cannot overcome all of these.

## The Pre-Frame Sequence

### Step 1: Story Page (Belief Shifter)
Send cold traffic first to a story-driven page that:
- Identifies their pain (makes them think "that's me")
- Introduces a new perspective
- Builds curiosity about the solution
- Does NOT sell anything yet

### Step 2: Bridge Content
Additional emails or pages that:
- Deepen the new belief
- Provide proof and examples
- Build anticipation

### Step 3: Offer Page
Only now, after beliefs have shifted, direct to the sales page.

## Why It Works

By the time prospects see the offer:
- They already believe the old way is broken
- They already trust your perspective
- They're curious about your solution
- The sale becomes a natural conclusion, not a pitch

## Chaperon's Key Insight

"Sometimes it's easier to just do something and see what happens." Don't overthink the funnel structure—test a simple story page before your offer and measure the difference.

## How to Apply

1. Identify the core belief that must shift for someone to buy
2. Create content that shifts that belief through story
3. Place that content between your traffic source and your offer
4. Measure conversion rate difference
