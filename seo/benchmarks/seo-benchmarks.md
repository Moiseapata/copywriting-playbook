# SEO Benchmarks

**Tags:** #seo #benchmarks #data

## Search Volume Distribution

- Google handles ~13.7 billion searches a day (Neil Patel).
- Google sends 190x more traffic to websites than ChatGPT (Matt Diggity).
- Google-style search represents only ~27% of all search-like activity on the internet; the other ~73% happens across Instagram, TikTok, Amazon, Reddit, YouTube, and AI assistants (Neil Patel).

## GBP / Local Profile Benchmarks

- Google allows up to 10 categories on a Google Business Profile; most businesses use only 1-2.
- Businesses should list 30+ services; most list only ~5.

## Content Production Benchmarks (AI-Agent Automation)

- Manual "Core 30" local page build: 1-2 hours/page, 45-60 hours per client.
- AI-agent automated build: 18 pages with videos in ~70 minutes.

## AI Search Adoption

- Google's AI Overviews reached over 1 billion users by the end of 2024 (Tim Cameron-Kitchen).

## Key Insight

Traditional ranking metrics (position, traffic volume) increasingly need to be paired with AI-visibility metrics (citation frequency, mention gaps) since Google Search Console cannot see AI-assistant referral traffic. See `ai-search-geo/ai-search-tracking.md`.
