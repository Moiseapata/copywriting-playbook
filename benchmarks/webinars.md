# Webinar Benchmarks

**Source:** Russell Brunson
**Tags:** #benchmarks #webinars #data

## Registration and Attendance (Paid Traffic)

| Metric | Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|
| Registration Rate | 30% — 50% of page visitors |
| Show-Up Rate | 20% — 30% of registrants |

## Close Rates (of Attendees at Pitch)

| Close Rate | Business Trajectory |
|------------|---------------------|
| 5% | Six-figure year |
| 10% | Multi-million dollar year |
| 15% | Billion-dollar trajectory |

## The Biggest Drop-Off Point

**The first 5 minutes.** If people don't feel the webinar is for them in the opening, they leave.

**Fix:** The 3-Person Survey. "Type 1 for beginner, 2 for intermediate, 3 for advanced." Then explain specifically how each group will benefit.

## Key Structural Elements

- **Intro (5 min):** 3-Person Survey + agenda
- **Epiphany Bridge Story (15 min):** Relatable before → discovery → the map
- **Teaching Section:** Strategy (what), not tactics (how)
- **Pitch:** Stack value, present offer, close with urgency

## The "What, Not How" Rule

Teaching tactical "how" kills sales. Teach the strategic framework. The paid product is where they get the exact implementation steps.
