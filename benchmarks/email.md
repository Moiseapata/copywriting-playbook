# Email Benchmarks

**Tags:** #benchmarks #email #data

## Industry Averages

### Open Rates
- Typical range: 17% — 28%
- Strong performers: 35%+
- Exceptional: 60% — 80% (with strong subject lines and engaged lists)

### Click-Through Rates (CTR)
- Average: 1% — 5%
- Strong: 8%+ (Hormozi's benchmark across 10M emails)
- **This is the primary metric to optimize for long-term** (open rates are increasingly unreliable due to privacy changes)

### ROI
- Average: $35 — $45 return for every $1 spent
- With segmentation: 791% increase in ROI (HubSpot)

## Consumption Data

- 61% of people check email multiple times per day
- 20% check it multiple times per hour
- Email consumption is nearly uniform across age groups (15-24 through 65+)
- 90% of people in every age bracket read email

## Best Send Times

| Business Type | Best Days | Best Times |
|---------------|-----------|------------|
| B2C | Monday, Tuesday | 10 AM — Noon, 1 PM — 3 PM |
| B2B | Wednesday | 10 AM — Noon, 1 PM — 3 PM |

## Deliverability Benchmarks

- 16.99% of B2B marketing emails never reach the inbox
- Plain text emails have significantly higher deliverability than HTML templates
- Google-native links (YouTube, Google Drive) have higher deliverability

## Key Insight

Open rates are increasingly unreliable due to privacy changes and image blocking. Click-through rate and reply rate are the more meaningful engagement metrics to track.
