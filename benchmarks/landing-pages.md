## Key Metrics to Track

- **Unique visitors** to landing page
- **Conversion rate** (opt-ins or sales / unique visitors)
- **Traffic source** performance (which sources convert best)
- **Return visitor rate** (Copy Posse found 60% of buyers visited twice before purchasing)

## Factors That Impact Conversion

### Above the Fold
- Headline clarity
- CTA visibility
- Above-fold form (no scrolling required to opt in)

### Page Design
- No navigation (standalone page)
- Single column
- Clean, simple design (Suby's "stop looking like a funnel hacker" principle)

### Trust Elements
- Social proof
- Authority indicators
- Privacy promises
- Secure badges

### Copy Quality
- Clear singular benefit
- Curiosity-driven headline
- Objection handling
- Specificity over vagueness

## The "Multiple Touch" Reality

People take longer to make buying decisions. Expect multiple visits before conversion. Build pages that reward return visits with clear, consistent messaging.
