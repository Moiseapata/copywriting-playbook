# Thank You Pages

**Source:** Amy Porterfield
**Tags:** #landing-pages #email #onboarding #porterfield

## The Golden Rule

**Do NOT deliver the freebie directly on the thank-you page.**

Do not put the PDF, video, or download link on the thank-you page.

## Why

The goal is to get your audience into the habit of opening your emails and trusting that your emails are valuable. This is crucial for when you eventually sell.

If you deliver everything on the thank-you page:
- They never need to open your emails
- They never build the email habit
- When you launch, they won't open your sales emails

## What to Do Instead

### On the Thank-You Page:
1. Confirm the sign-up worked
2. Express gratitude
3. Guide them to check their email
4. Add a teaser: "Don't skip page five, it's my favorite tip!"

### In the Email:
1. Deliver the lead magnet
2. Set expectations for future emails
3. Start building the relationship

## The Teaser Tip

"Don't skip page five, it's my favorite tip!" works because:
- Piques curiosity
- Ensures they open the email
- Ensures they consume the content
- Trains the email-opening habit

## The Landing Page URL

Keep it simple and memorable: `yourdomain.com/leads` or `yourdomain.com/freebie-name`

## Tools

- **Landing Page Builders:** Leadpages (battle-tested templates), ConvertKit (built-in ESP integration)
- **Pop-ups:** Squarespace built-in, Popup Ally (less intrusive)
