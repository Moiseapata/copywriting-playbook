# Email Deliverability

**Source:** Alex Hormozi, Andre Chaperon, Tyson 4D
**Tags:** #email #deliverability #segmentation #list-hygiene

## Core Principle

If your emails don't reach the inbox, nothing else matters. Deliverability is the foundation.

## The Unsubscribe Mindset Shift

**Unsubscribes are not an insult.** They're a feature.

### Why Unsubscribes Help
- Uninterested people who stay on your list go dormant
- Dormant subscribers lower your domain deliverability
- Lower deliverability means interested people don't receive your emails

**Vanity metrics harm real performance.**

### "Shaking the Tree"
The first email to a cold list will have higher-than-normal unsubscribes. This is "pulling forward churn"—the people who would unsubscribe eventually do it now, saving your deliverability.

**Hormozi's numbers:** First email had just over 1% unsubscribe. Now 0.42%.

## Segmentation and Deliverability

HubSpot research: 791% increase in email ROI from list segmentation.

**Why it matters for deliverability:**
- Relevant emails get opened
- Opened emails signal engagement
- Engagement improves sender reputation
- Better reputation = more inbox placement

## Plain Text Principle

**Your email should look like a regular email between colleagues**, not a promotional flyer.

### Why
- Email algorithms (AI) filter promotional-looking emails into the Promotions tab
- For B2B, 16.99% of marketing emails never reach the inbox
- Plain text signals human-to-human interaction

### The "Inbox vs. Vegas Strip" Rule
Your email should look like it belongs in the inbox—mostly text, a handful of sentences, maybe a link. It should NOT look like a desperate sales flyer with multiple fonts, colors, and images.

## Advanced Tactic: Google-Native Links

Use links to Google-native domains (YouTube videos, Google Drive documents). These have higher deliverability because email servers recognize and trust the domains.

## Re-engagement Strategy

Periodically run re-engagement campaigns:
- "We noticed you haven't been opening emails. If you still want to hear from us, click here."
- Non-responders should be removed or suppressed
- This protects deliverability for engaged subscribers

## Key Tracking Note

Open tracking is increasingly unreliable (privacy changes, image blocking). Click tracking and reply tracking are more reliable engagement signals.

## Common Mistakes That Hurt Deliverability

1. Changing sender names (breaks trust and recognition)
2. Sending irrelevant emails to entire list
3. Overloading with low-value content
4. Keeping dormant subscribers indefinitely
