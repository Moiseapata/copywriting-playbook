# Opt-In Page Template

**Tags:** #template #landing-page #opt-in #lead-magnet

## Structure

A simple, conversion-optimized page for capturing email addresses in exchange for a lead magnet.

---

## Section 1: Above the Fold

**Headline:** [Clear benefit of the lead magnet]
*Example: "Get 100 Subject Lines That Drive More Clicks and Engagement"*

**Subheadline:** [What it is + why they should want it]
*Example: "The Subject Line Split Test Report — free download"*

**Visual:** Hero image showing the lead magnet (mockup of the PDF, report cover, etc.)

**CTA:** [Simple form — Name + Email + Button]
*Button text: "Send Me the Report" or "Get Instant Access"*

**Trust Element:** Small text near form: "We respect your privacy. Unsubscribe anytime."

**Authority Banner (optional):** "As seen on [logos]" or "[X]+ downloads"

---

## Section 2: What's Inside

**Headline:** "Here's What You'll Get:"

**Bullets:**
- [Specific component 1 + benefit]
- [Specific component 2 + benefit]
- [Specific component 3 + benefit]
- [Specific component 4 + benefit]

---

## Section 3: Brief Authority/Trust

**Short Bio:**
"Hi, I'm [Name]. I've [credibility statement — results, experience, clients]."

**Optional:** Small headshot

---

## Section 4: Final CTA

**Headline:** "Ready to [benefit]?"

**CTA Button:** [Same as above fold]

**Trust Reminder:** "[X]+ people have already downloaded this. Join them."

---

## Design Rules (Suby)

- No navigation menu (standalone page)
- Black text on white/light background
- Single column layout
- Above the fold: headline, benefit, CTA — no scrolling required
- Remove everything that isn't essential to the conversion

## Delivery Rule (Porterfield)

**Do NOT deliver the freebie on this page.** Direct them to a thank-you page that tells them to check their email. Train the email habit.
