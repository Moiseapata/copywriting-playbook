# Welcome Sequence Template

**Tags:** #template #email #welcome #onboarding

## Structure

A 3-email sequence that onboards new subscribers, delivers the lead magnet, and sets expectations.

---

### Email 1: Welcome + Deliver (Immediate)

**Subject Line:** [Lead magnet name] — here's your access

**Body:**

> Hey [First Name],
>
> Welcome! You're in.
>
> Here's your [lead magnet name]: [link]
>
> Inside you'll find:
> - [Bullet 1 — specific, benefit-driven]
> - [Bullet 2]
> - [Bullet 3]
>
> Quick tip: Don't skip [specific part]. It's my favorite piece and where most people get their first breakthrough.
>
> Here's what to expect from me:
> - I send [X] emails per week on [topics]
> - My goal is to help you [outcome]
> - Hit reply anytime — I read every response
>
> Talk soon,
> [Name]
>
> P.S. [Reinforce one key point or add a curiosity teaser for next email]

---

### Email 2: Pure Value (Day 2-3)

**Subject Line:** The [number] thing I wish I knew about [topic]

**Body:**

> Hey [First Name],
>
> When I was starting out with [topic], I made every mistake possible.
>
> The biggest one? [Specific mistake]
>
> Here's what I learned: [Core lesson — valuable and specific]
>
> [Short explanation or story that teaches the lesson]
>
> The takeaway: [One clear, actionable insight]
>
> Tomorrow I'm sending something that might surprise you.
>
> [Name]
>
> P.S. [Reinforce the lesson or add context]

---

### Email 3: Value + Soft CTA (Day 4-5)

**Subject Line:** A quick question for you

**Body:**

> Hey [First Name],
>
> I've been thinking about [topic] and wanted to share something.
>
> [Valuable insight or story]
>
> By the way — I do have a [product/service] that helps people with exactly this. It's called [name] and it [core benefit].
>
> No pressure at all. Just wanted you to know it exists if you're ever looking for more hands-on help.
>
> Either way, I'll be back in your inbox [next send day] with more [value promise].
>
> [Name]
>
> P.S. Hit reply and tell me: what's your biggest struggle with [topic] right now? I read every response and it helps me know what to cover next.

---

## Rules

- All emails from a personal sender name (not a company)
- Plain text format (not HTML templates)
- Short paragraphs (1-3 lines)
- One clear goal per email
- Value first, ask later
