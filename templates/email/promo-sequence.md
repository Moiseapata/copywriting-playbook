# Promo Sequence Template

**Tags:** #template #email #promo #sales

## Structure

A multi-email promotional sequence using the PAS framework and deadline urgency.

---

### Email 1: Value + Hard CTA

**Subject Line:** [Curiosity or benefit-driven]

**Body:**

> Hey [First Name],
>
> [Problem statement — relate to their pain]
>
> [Agitate — the cost of not fixing this]
>
> [Insight/Value — teach something genuinely useful]
>
> This is exactly why I created [product].
>
> [One-line description of product and core benefit]
>
> [Specific result or social proof — one sentence]
>
> If you want to [outcome], here's what to do: [CTA link]
>
> [Name]
>
> P.S. [Reinforce urgency or key benefit]

---

### Email 2: Scarcity/Deadline (24 Hours)

**Subject Line:** 24 hours left [Action Required]

**Body:**

> Hey [First Name],
>
> In case you missed it, [offer] closes in 24 hours.
>
> [Link]
>
> Quick recap of what you get:
> - [Bullet 1]
> - [Bullet 2]
> - [Bullet 3]
>
> But only if you take action in the next 24 hours. The clock is ticking.
>
> [Name]
>
> P.S. [Final push — why waiting is a bad idea]

---

### Email 3: Final Deadline (1 Hour)

**Subject Line:** 1 hour left. Now or never.

**Body:**

> [First Name],
>
> Last call.
>
> [Offer] closes in 60 minutes.
>
> [Link]
>
> After that, [what happens — price goes up, doors close, etc.]
>
> Don't miss this.
>
> [Name]

---

## Rules for Promo Sequences

- Use real deadlines (never fake scarcity)
- Keep emails focused on one goal: the click
- Segment: only send to people who haven't purchased
- Track clicks and remove buyers from sequence
- Test subject lines on small segment before full send
