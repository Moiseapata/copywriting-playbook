# 24-Hour Deadline Email Structure

**Source:** Tyson 4D
**Tags:** #template #email #deadline #urgency

## The Template

Use this for the final 24 hours of any promotion.

---

### Email 1: 24 Hours Remaining

**Subject Line:** 24 hours left [Action Required]

**Body:**

> Hey [First Name],
>
> In case you missed it, [the offer] closes in 24 hours.
>
> [Direct link to offer]
>
> You'll get:
> - [Core benefit 1]
> - [Core benefit 2]
> - [Core benefit 3]
>
> But only if you take action in the next 24 hours. The clock is ticking.
>
> Talk soon,
> [Name]

---

### Email 2: Morning Of (Sent Early)

**Subject Line:** [Offer] closes tonight at midnight

**Body:**

> [First Name],
>
> Final reminder.
>
> [Link]
>
> After midnight, [what changes — price goes up, enrollment closes, etc.].
>
> If you've been on the fence, now's the time.
>
> [Name]

---

### Email 3: Final Hour

**Subject Line:** 1 hour left. Now or never.

**Body:**

> [First Name],
>
> Last call.
>
> [Link]
>
> In 60 minutes, this offer disappears.
>
> Don't wake up tomorrow wishing you'd acted today.
>
> [Name]

---

## The Psychology

- **Multiple touches:** 3-4 emails on the final day is normal and effective
- **Massive percentage of launch revenue often comes on the final day**
- **Clear, urgent, and direct** — no new information, just reminders and urgency
- **"Now or never" framing** triggers loss aversion

## When to Send

- Email 1: 24 hours before close (morning)
- Email 2: Morning of close
- Email 3: 1-2 hours before close
- Optional Email 4: 15 minutes before close
